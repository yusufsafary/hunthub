❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything founders ask us before launching their startup.

How often is this list updated? ▼
HuntHub is updated every week. We add new platforms, remove inactive ones, verify links, and refresh trending product data every 7 days. The banner at the top always shows the exact last update date.
Should I submit to all 107 platforms at once? ▼
No. We recommend starting with 5–10 top-tier platforms like Product Hunt, Hacker News, and BetaList. Stagger submissions over 2–4 weeks, engage with each community, and track which ones drive conversions before moving to smaller directories.
Are all these platforms free to submit to? ▼
About 80% are free. Some platforms like AppSumo, StackSocial, and RocketHub are revenue-sharing or paid models for featuring deals. Each card is tagged with FREE, PAID, or LTD to make it easy to filter by budget.
What is a Lifetime Deal (LTD) platform? ▼
LTD platforms like AppSumo and StackSocial let you sell your software with a one-time payment instead of a subscription. It's a fast way to generate early revenue and a large user base, but typically requires revenue sharing with the platform.
Can I suggest a platform that's not listed? ▼
Absolutely. Use the newsletter form below to reach us and suggest a platform. If it meets our quality criteria — active community, real traffic, working submission process — we'll add it in the next weekly update.
What is orynth.dev and why is it on this list? ▼
Orynth.dev is a curated startup discovery platform that has been growing rapidly. It's particularly strong for developer tools and Web3 products. We've placed it at #20 based on its growing community, quality curation, and strong engagement metrics in 2026.
Does submitting to directories actually help with SEO? ▼
Yes, significantly. A listing on Product Hunt, AlternativeTo, or SaaSHub generates high-authority backlinks that pass real ranking signals to your domain. Many founders see measurable organic traffic improvement within 30–60 days of a multi-platform launch.
